The Heritage Dining Table

Wood Species
Black Walnut
Location
Aspen, Colorado
Dimensions
120"L × 44"W × 30"H

Commissioned for a newly built architectural mountain home, the Heritage Dining Table was designed to be the undisputed focal point of a large, open-concept living space. Needs commanded a piece that could comfortably seat ten, yet maintain a visual lightness that wouldn't dominate the room.

We sourced two sequential slabs of sustainably harvested Black Walnut from an old-growth tree. By bookmatching the slabs, we created a mesmerizing symmetrical grain pattern down the center seam, secured with traditional hand-cut butterfly joints using contrasting maple wood.
