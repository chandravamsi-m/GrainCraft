Frequently Asked Questions

What is the typical lead time for a custom piece?

Our lead times typically range from 8 to 12 weeks depending on the complexity of the design and our current commission queue. Small shop items ship within 3-5 business days.

Do you ship nationwide?

Yes, we offer white-glove delivery across the contiguous United States using our trusted freight partners. Crating and shipping fees are calculated during the final quote process.
