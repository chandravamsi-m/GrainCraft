Materials & Ethos

We believe in materials that age with grace. Our studio exclusively works with sustainable hardwoods and non-toxic, natural oil finishes.

Heirloom Stewardship

Every piece of wood that enters our studio is traced back to its origin. We partner with local arborists and certified sustainable forests to ensure that our work contributes to the health of our woodlands, not their depletion.

Our Library

Species of Choice

Reclaimed White Oak

Known for its strength and prominent ray fleck pattern. Our white oak is often reclaimed from heritage structures, giving it a depth of character that new growth cannot match.

DurabilityExcellent
Color RangeAmber to Pale Gold
Recommended ForDining Tables, Flooring

American Black Walnut

The crown jewel of American hardwoods. Walnut is prized for its rich, dark heartwood and complex swirling grain patterns. It develops a lustrous patina over time.

DurabilityGood
Color RangeDeep Chocolate to Cream
Recommended ForDesks, Credenzas

Wild Cherry

A wood that literally ripens. Cherry begins as a warm salmon-pink and deepens into a rich, reddish-brown as it is exposed to sunlight. It is exceptionally smooth to the touch.

DurabilityMedium
Color RangePink to Deep Red-Brown
Recommended ForCabinets, Small Goods
