Sustainably Sourced

The foundation of quality.

A piece of furniture is only as resilient as the timber it's carved from. We select only the highest grade domestic hardwoods.

Black Walnut

Renowned for its deep, rich chocolate hues and striking grain patterns. Walnut ages gracefully, developing a warm patina over time.

White Oak

A cornerstone of traditional and modern furniture alike. Exceptionally strong, water-resistant, and features a clean, tight grain structure.

Hard Maple

Bright, smooth, and dense. Maple is highly resistant to wear and brings an airy, contemporary lightness to any interior environment.
