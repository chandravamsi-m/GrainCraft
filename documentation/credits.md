# Page Structure

The template follows a consistent semantic structure for all pages.

## Layout Components
- **Navbar**: Sticky at the top with theme toggle and navigation.
- **Banner/Hero**: Page-specific introductory section.
- **Main Section**: Content areas with `.section` and `.container` wrappers.
- **Footer**: Common bottom navigation and studio info.

## Key Page Templates
- `index.html`: Studio Portfolio Homepage.
- `about.html`: Narrative "Maker Story" page.
- `portfolio.html`: Filterable category grid.
- `materials.html`: Tabbed material showcase.
- `process.html`: Staggered step-by-step methodology.
- `shop.html`: Catalog-style product showcase.
- `contact.html`: Standard contact with form and map.
- `quote.html`: Advanced multi-step project catalog & inquiry form.
